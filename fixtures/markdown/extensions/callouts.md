# Callouts

> [!note] A plain note callout
> Body of the note.

> [!summary] Alias resolves to abstract
> Summary body.

> [!warning]- Folded warning
> Hidden until expanded.

> [!tip]+ Expanded tip
> Shown by default.

> [!danger]
> No title danger.

> [!quirky-custom] Custom type
> Renders with default icon.

> [!question] Outer question
> > [!todo]- Nested folded todo
> > Inner body.
